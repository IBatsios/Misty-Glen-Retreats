This is a new vacation rental house on gated property in a remote corner of Ashe County, North Carolina. The house was completed in September 2019 and is elegantly furnished inside and out.

The 18 foot vaulted ceiling great room has a wood burning fireplace. The main floor is elevated in the trees, with covered decks and mountain views. The south deck has dining seating for eight, and the west deck has very plush and comfortable seating for entertaining and relaxation. Stairs lead from the west deck sitting area to a cedar hot tub, nestled in the forest.

The kitchen is designed for cooking and appointed with all necessary tools. There is indoor dining seating for eight.

The ground level features "The Getaway", a room with a big screen TV, a large modern sectional sofa, and a small refrigerator. Vintage neon signs and tin ceiling tiles create a really cool place to "getaway". Outside this room is a covered area for entertaining and dining, complete with a gas grill and built in seating, and a picnic table.
